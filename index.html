Dr. Daya, is currently appointed as Assistant Professor in Indian Institute of Technology. He has more than 9 Years of experience. He has also Worked as Assistant Professor at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Jais, Amethi UP., Worked as Assistant Professor at Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering & Technology Longowal, Punjab, Worked as Assistant Professor at Shershah Engineering College Sasaram, Bihar under Govt. of Bihar, Worked as Assistant Professor at Loknayak Jai Prakash Institute of Technology Chapra, Bihar under NPIU TEQIP III, and Worked as Teaching Assistant (TA) at IIT(ISM), Dhanbad.
He published over thirty-five research articles and was a reviewer in many reputed (inter)national journals and conferences. He served as a session chair and has given invited talks at several reputed conferences, and served as a reviewer/TPC member in a couple of highly reputed international journals/conferences. Besides, he is a senior member of IEEE and a Professional Member of ACM.
He is the IEEE Senior Member and in research, his area of involvements are Applied Cryptography, Post-quantum Cryptography, IoT security, Blockchain, Hardware Security. He has published more than thirty-five research articles including International Journals, International Conferences and Book chapter. He has served as a reviewer in many reputed journals (1. Science China Information Science, Springer (SCI-E). ), also serve as editorial board member in a journals(Information Engineering and Applied Computing.), held the position of track chair in a conferences( Third International Conference on Cryptography, Network Security and Blockchain technology (ICNSBT 2024) India), and also be a part of program committe member of many conferences(1. Congress on Intelligent Systems (CIS 2020) India. 2. The 8th IEEE International Conference on Smart City and Informatization (iSCI 2020) China. 3.
