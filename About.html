Laboratory of Applied Cryptology and Security (LACS)

 
 
 
 
 
LACS is the career building lab for research in the field of security. In this, we work in many dimension of security like Post quantum cryptography, lattice, blockchain, digital signature, puf, RSA, DSA, ECC and many more. LACS can serve as an invaluable resource for students interested in the field of cybersecurity and information assurance. Security is major concern in this digital era which includes computer security, cybersecurity, digital security, or information technology security (IT security) and many more. our major concern to protect with various attacks, some of are:
  • Vulnerabilities and attacks
  • Backdoor
  • Denial-of-service attack
  • Direct-access attacks
  • Eavesdropping
  • Malware
  • Man-in-the-middle attacks
  • Multi-vector, polymorphic attacks
  • Phishing
  • Privilege escalation
  • Side-channel attack
  • Social engineering
  • Spoofing
  • Tampering
  • HTML smuggling
  • Types of security and privacy
  • Access control
  • Anti-keyloggers
  • Anti-malware
  • Anti-spyware
  • Anti-subversion software
  • Anti-tamper software
  • Anti-theft
  • Antivirus softwar
  • Security information and event management (SIEM)
  • Software and operating system updating
  • Vulnerability Management
